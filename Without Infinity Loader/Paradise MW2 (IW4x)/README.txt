* Navigate to your iw4x install directory (C:\Program Files (x86)\Steam\steamapps\common\Call of Duty Modern Warfare 2)

* Drop the "Paradise" folder into your mods folder (create a "mods" folder if you dont already have one)

* Launch iw4x and click on mods, double click on "Paradise" from the menu on the left

* IW4x should close and open a new window

* Click on "Create a Game" and pick game mode/map

* Then simply start the game!
